# Architecturally Significant Requirements (ASR)

Architecturally Significant Requirements (ASRs) are those requirements that have a measurable impact on a software system's architecture. They dictate the choices we make for the structure, behavior, and operation of our applications across all projects.

This document serves as an index and summary of all recorded ASRs expected to be implemented within our organization.

The Confluence [version](https://equisoft.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/HRMI/pages/52232254/ASR) is a read-only copy of the ASRs in the [standards repository](https://github.com/kronostechnologies/standards/tree/master/asr). Changes to ASRs should be made in the repository and will be automatically reflected in Confluence.
